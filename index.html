Chayan Kumar Saha

PhD Candidate · Department of Molecular Biology · Umeå University · Sweden

Hi and welcome!

I am a final year Bioinformatics PhD student in the group of Gemma Catherine Atkinson. The focus of my doctoral research is the functional evolution of RelA/SpoT Homologue enzymes which produce the alarmone nucleotide (p)ppGpp, that modulates many different aspects of bacterial physiology.

I have developed a python programming tool FlaGs (www.webflags.se) that can analyse conservation of gene neighbourhood during evolution (PMID: 32956448). Using FlaGs, we have discovered that some homologues of (p)ppGpp synthesising enzymes known as SASs (Small Alarmone Sythetases) can be toxins in a novel toxin-antitoxin system (PMID: 32345719). Currently, I am trying to uncover the complete network of toxin and antitoxin domain combinations.
